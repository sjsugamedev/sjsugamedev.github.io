Creative Game Jam 2020

Join the Creative Game Jam hosted by the CADRE media labs in collaboration with the Game Dev Club at SJSU

This will be a week long challenge to think outside of the box or solve a thrilling problem. We are hosting this to provide a creative outlet for those seeking one. We welcome any who is looking to learn a new tool or wants to connect with other student game developers. Through this jam, we want to support new and experienced student developers to create innovative projects. Secondly, we want to build a stronger community by connecting students together.

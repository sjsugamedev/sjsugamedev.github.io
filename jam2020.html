Creative Game Jam 2020

Join the Creative Game Jam hosted by the CADRE media labs in collaboration with the Game Dev Club at SJSU

This will be a week long challenge to think outside of the box or solve a thrilling problem. Through this jam, we want to support new and experienced student developers to create innovative projects. During this time, participants will concept an idea, develop the game or other project, test for bugs, make revisions, and finally share their project at the end. This also a great opportunity to learn a new tool or dive deeper into a familiar one.

What can you expect?

  • Finish with a new game on your porfolio (Awesome!).
  • Learn a new tool or a practical skill.
  • Connect with other student developers!

Will this be your chance to get started in game development?

We encourage anyone who wants to start making games to try a game jam. In a short amount of time, newcomers can become familiar with a game engine and leave with a finished game. If you're new, join the Game Dev Club Discord to get connected with other students. There are channels to request help and get valuable feedback.

Workshops

We believe failure is a necessary step for learning so that is why we are hosting a workshop on time management for developing games.

For those looking to learn a new tool, we will host an introductory workshop on Twine.

How do I join?

We are inviting designers, artists, programmers, engineers, writers, and other creatives to come together and create something. Although we are advertising this as a game jam, we welcome any other creations!

If you’re looking for a team, say so in the form and we will help you find others to collaborate with.

About Us

The Game Development Club was founded to serve as an environment for people to learn about the process of developing games, to play and understand game design, and to collaborate with others in researching and creating their own games.

What We Provide

By providing tools, resources, and many forms of inspiration and motivation we help people learn about the game development process regardless of their level of experience. We also participate in game jams and reach out to industry professionals who can elaborate on their experiences and teach fundamental game making ideas to our club.

Where We Meet

We meet every Thursday at 7:00 PM at the MLK Library's Innovation Center, located on the Lower Level (the basement level) on our Discord Server. Everyone is welcome, even non-students!

Elevator
  • Go to the elevators and hit the LL button, we’ll be to the right after exiting. We’ll be immediately on your right when entering the Innovation Centre.
Stairs
  • From the ground floor go to the stairs next to the elevator and go down to the very bottom of the stairs. From there go through the doorway and turn right past the elevators. We’ll be immediately on your right when entering the Innovation Centre

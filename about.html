About Us

The Game Development Club was created for the sole purpose of generating and maintaining an space for game development and game design. To foster a learning environment for game development and design where people can come together to meet others who share similar ineterests, to ocasionally play games for learning and research, and, above all, to learn how to develop games. We provide many resources, as well as inspiration and motivation, to help our members learn about the processes and roles that are put into game development. No prior game developing knowledge, such as programming or art experience, is necessary. The club is met to help and teach one another, so we will help you get started on your journey towards game development. All you need is passion, a positive attitude, and determination. Everyone and anyone who has a passion for games and game development or design is welcome to join us, even non-students.

Activities and Opportunities

We form small teams amongst the members to help them work on a game developing project centered on a agreed upon game idea within that team. In addition to that, the club hosts many events for our members to network with their peers and professionals to help them make meaninful connections. The club reaches out to industry professionals to have them explain and elaborate on their experiences in developing and designing games as well as to teach fundamental game making ideas. We also host fun game jams, where participants compete to try and make a video game from scratch within a limited amount of time. Come see our club game showcases to see some in-progress work and all the wonderful games that our members have created. All of this is done in hopes of helping you understand the process of game developing.
